Dog Care & Health Services

We care for your furry friends! Check out our list of common dog illnesses and their recommended treatments.

Fleas & Lice

Symptoms: Scratching, biting skin, visible fleas or eggs.

Recommended Medicine: Frontline Plus, NexGard, or Simparica.

Itchiness / Allergies

Symptoms: Redness, constant licking or paw biting.

Recommended Medicine: Apoquel, Cytopoint, or oatmeal-based shampoo.

Swelling / Inflammation

Symptoms: Puffy areas on the body, discomfort, limping.

Recommended Medicine: Carprofen, Meloxicam, or a vet-approved anti-inflammatory.

Ticks / Mites

Symptoms: Skin irritation, hair loss, or scabs.

Recommended Medicine: Bravecto, Revolution, or Selamectin.

Ear Infections

Symptoms: Head shaking, ear odor, or discharge.

Recommended Medicine: Otomax or Zymox ear drops.
